    • Component to the view

    Directives




    Angular transforms the DOM according to the instructions given by directives.


    A directive is a class with a @Directive() decorator


    Components are technically a directive


    Directives




    There are two specific directives


    • Structural

    • Attribute



    Structural Directives


    Replace element in the DOM conditionally with ngIf


    • Component exports: age = 19;

    • Template code: <p *ngIf="age < 21">

      Check out our great selection of mocktails!

      </p>

    Structural Directives


    Replace element in the DOM conditionally with ngFor


    • Component exports: drinks = ['Margarita', 'Cosmopolitan', 'Long Island', 'Beer'];

    • Template code: <ul><li *ngFor="let drink of drinks">

      {{drink}}

      </li></ul>



    Attribute Directives








    Only modifies the behavior or appearance of an element that already exists on the DOM
